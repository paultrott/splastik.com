About Me

I am a disabled independent painter based in Australia, and a single parent of two children who inspire me every day. My path as an artist has been shaped by years of work in graphic design, model making and scene creation for animation, online editorial content and television.

What drives me most is my passion for vehicle illustration, with a particular focus on military aviation subjects. From an early age, I was drawn to aircraft; spending every spare moment around them as a teenager. The works of James Dietz and Jaroslaw Wrobel showed me how art could capture the spirit of aviation, and they continue to influence my own approach to painting.

My work has found appreciation among veterans and enthusiasts who share this love of history and machinery. Looking ahead, I hope to bring my illustrations to a wider audience through books, magazines, and private collections. My goal is to create artwork that resonates with collectors in Australia, the UK, New Zealand, Canada, and the USA, and to ensure each piece carries both technical precision and a genuine love for the subject.
