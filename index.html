Process

From brief to final artwork: a transparent, hybrid workflow balancing AI exploration with human craft.

  1. Initial Brief & Discovery

    • Client supplies goals, specs, references (tone, format, audience, deadlines).
    • Clarrification goals:
      • Editorial vs. advertising intent
      • Deliverables (single, series, variants)
      • Brand guardrails & constraints
    • Optional: quick moodboard or verbal sketch to energize direction.

  2. Concept Development (LLM + Human Insight)

    • LLMs surface metaphors, narrative angles, stylistic frames.
    • Draft 2–3 conceptual directions + rough visual notes.
    • Share, refine lightly, lock direction pre‑synthesis.

  3. Image Synthesis (Stable Diffusion)

    • Generate studies tuned for composition, light, mood.
    • Align style (painterly, photoreal, surreal, hybrid).
    • Refine prompt logic / model parameters.
    • Curate strongest candidates for decision.

  4. Refinement & Compositing (Adobe + Krita)

    • Cleanup, masking, precise layer separation.
    • Generative fill / canvas extensions for layout agility.
    • Color grading & fidelity checks (print / digital).
    • Add typography / layout elements as required.

  5. Final Delivery

    • Export production‑ready assets:
      • Layered PSD / TIFF (editorial)
      • Flattened hi‑res JPEG / PNG (ads)
      • Optional web / social variants
    • Include metadata, licensing & usage guidance.
    • Handoff via preferred method (cloud / email / DAM).

  6. Optional Revisions & Support

    • One round of minor tweaks (color, crop, overlay) included.
    • Further revisions billed hourly or per scope.
    • Ongoing support for campaigns or serialized editorial.

Capabilities

A diffusion designer combines art direction, narrative strategy, and automation‑first image synthesis to deliver brand‑faithful visuals at speed. Drawing on an MFA in Digital Media (RISD) and a PgDip in Creative Writing (Birkbeck), this practice unites rigorous visual R&D with clear, persuasive communication—equally comfortable in editorial, advertising, and experiential contexts.

Core Creative Capabilities

  • Concept development & narrative strategy:
    Translates briefs into coherent story worlds, themes, and stylistic guardrails; frames concepts with treatments, loglines, and visual metaphors aligned to brand voice & campaign objectives.
  • Visual R&D with diffusion systems:
    Explores composition, light, color, and material language via controlled promptcraft, seed mapping, and parameter sweeps; builds variant matrices that chart range without idea drift.
  • Art direction & stylistic fidelity:
    Creates reference‑informed lookbooks & style bibles; enforces consistency through controlled inputs (prompts, negatives, samplers) & curated model selections.
  • Editorial & campaign fluency:
    Designs covers, feature spreads, OOH & social variants with hierarchy, pacing, legibility; integrates copy / CTA placements without diluting visual intent.
  • Advanced compositing & finishing:
    Precise masking, plate cleanup, generative extension, relighting, nuanced grading; balances painterly and photoreal modes & preserves edge integrity at large formats.
  • Color management & output fidelity:
    Calibrated workflows, ICC soft‑proofing, optimized web/sRGB delivery; predictable color across substrates & devices.
  • Writing for clarity & persuasion:
    Briefs, rationales, proposals, and user docs; injects narrative coherence so teams execute with confidence.

Creative

Automation‑First Pipeline Engineering

  • Modular workflows:
    Reusable, parameterized Stable Diffusion / ComfyUI graphs enabling rapid branching & controlled randomness.
  • Reproducibility & audit trails:
    Captures seeds, prompts, model hashes & metadata directly in assets; layered PSDs with smart objects & non‑destructive adjustments.
  • Batch production & coverage:
    Automated aspect adaptations, padding, generative fill; permutation tests maximize market / language / placement coverage.
  • Toolchain integration:
    Bridges Photoshop scripting, AutoHotkey & metadata utilities; aligns with Figma / Adobe CC libraries & CMS needs.
  • Performance & reliability:
    Anticipates edge cases, designs graceful degradation & recovery; automated quality checks (resolution, color space, safe areas).
